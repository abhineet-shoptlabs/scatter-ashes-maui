Embodying Kokua

Compassionate Support Every Step of the Way

At Scatter Ashes Maui, we handle all the details of your loved one's Celebration of Life so you don't have to. Kokua is a Hawaiian word meaning "help", "aid" or "assistance". It embodies the spirit of lending a hand or offering support to others in need of it. Discover peace of mind with our personalized, full service care embodying the spirit of Kokua - offering support every step of the way with warmth, understanding, and respect.

How It All Began

Ceremonies Crafted with Love

"Over the span of decades, I've lovingly crafted ceremonies as sacred celebrations of life, feeling the presence of each cherished soul..."

In the gentle tradewinds of Maui, amidst the whispers of the sea and the songs of the land, Scatter Ashes Maui was born. Inspired by the aloha spirit and sacred traditions of these islands, we envisioned a sanctuary of peace and comfort for those seeking to honor their beloved departed.

With every ceremony, we embrace the essence of kokua, offering compassionate care, meticulous attention to detail, and a Full-Service approach to ensure every aspect of the journey is handled with utmost professionalism.
